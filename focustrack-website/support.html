Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking my workouts?

Open FocusTrack, tap the + button, and select "New Workout". Choose your workout type and follow the prompts to start tracking.

How do I connect my Apple Watch?

Install FocusTrack on your Apple Watch through the Watch app on your iPhone. Open the app on both devices and follow the sync instructions.

How do I track my nutrition?

Use the nutrition tab to log meals. You can search our database, scan barcodes, or create custom entries.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions, find FocusTrack, and tap Cancel Subscription.
