Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FocusTrack

Getting Started

How do I start tracking my workouts?

Open FocusTrack, tap the + button, and select "New Workout". Choose your workout type and follow the prompts to start tracking. Our AI will automatically detect your exercises and provide form feedback.

Can I connect my Apple Watch?

Yes! Install FocusTrack on your Apple Watch through the Watch app on your iPhone. The app will automatically sync your workouts, heart rate, and activity data.

Features & Functionality

How does the AI form detection work?

Our advanced AI analyzes your movement patterns through your device's camera to provide real-time feedback on your exercise form, helping prevent injuries and maximize results.

What types of workouts can I track?

FocusTrack supports a wide range of activities including strength training, cardio, HIIT, yoga, and more. You can also create custom workout types.

Subscription & Billing

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Find FocusTrack and tap "Cancel Subscription".

What's included in the Family Plan?

The Family Plan includes all Premium features for up to 6 family members, shared progress tracking, family challenges, and a family dashboard.

Data & Privacy

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your personal information is never shared without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your workout and nutrition data at any time in various formats (CSV, PDF) through the app's settings menu.

We're here to help! Reach out to our support team.
