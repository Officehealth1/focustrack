Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2025

Introduction

At FocusTrack, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains our minimal data collection approach and how we protect your information when you use our mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

We maintain a strict minimal data collection policy. The only personal information we collect is:

  • Your email address (for account creation and communication)

All workout data and app preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Email

Your email address is used exclusively for:

  • Account authentication
  • Essential service notifications
  • Responding to your support requests

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties.

Data Security

We protect your email address using industry-standard security measures:

  • Secure, encrypted storage
  • Limited access by authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your email from our system
  • Update your email address
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

Local Data Storage

All your workout data, preferences, and other app-related information are stored locally on your device. This means:

  • Your fitness data never leaves your device
  • You have complete control over your data
  • You can delete your data at any time through the app settings
